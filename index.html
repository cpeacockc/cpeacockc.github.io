I'm a theoretical physicist interested in understanding the behavior of quantum many-body systems. My research explores the conditions under which systems thermalize, obeying statistical mechanics, or evade thermalization through mechanisms such as integrability and localization due to disorder. I study phase transitions, non-equilibrium dynamics, and ergodicity breaking in both open and closed systems. To this end, I creatively develop and apply analytical and numerical techniques for simulating quantum systems on classical computers.

Research Interests & Expertise

Theoretical Condensed Matter Physics
Quantum chaos and integrability; Non-equilibrium dynamics; Localization and disordered systems; Dissipative and driven dynamics
Quantum Simulation Methods
Tensor networks; Krylov subspace methods; Pauli string binary encoding; Global optimization; Quantum trajectories
